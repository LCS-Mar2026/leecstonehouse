Proprietary Framework

The People Risk
Intelligence Framework

The PRIF is a six-layer workforce intelligence architecture designed to give executive leadership and boards real-time visibility of people risk, conduct patterns, and governance compliance, in the same analytical language that finance and risk functions have spoken for decades.

Not a product. An operating model, deployable within a single quarter in any regulated or high-growth business, built on standard enterprise tooling with no proprietary software lock-in.
